LANCE BROWN

PERSONAL REAL ESTATE CORPORATION

We build your real estate goals together. We crush those goals together. We work hard to align your real estate and financial goals with the lifestyle you are looking to achieve.
That is our focus when we work together.

We get these results by tapping into my decade of experience as a Realtor, a Managing Broker, and a real estate investor.
Market knowledge, negotiation strategies, extensive contract development, and marketing innovation are what will I bring that will help you reach your goals.

I find success comes naturally when relationships are valued and there is a focused commitment in our work together.
Tremendous wealth can be created from building a real estate portfolio - let me show you how in a way this is authentic to you.


Lance lives in Ocean Park, South Surrey with his wife, daughter and son. The family enjoys frequent visits to Fun Fun Park, hiking local trails in Semiahmoo, swimming at Grandview pool, playing at the South Surrey Rec Centre, and doing brunch at the Wooden Spoon.
Born and raised in Penticton, BC, Lance entered the real estate industry in Vancouver in 2006. His career has taken him to the position of Managing Broker for Western Canada’s largest real estate marketing firm, but his passion lies with the daily interaction he has with his clients and building meaningful working relationships.

CASE STUDY - MARKETING RESULTS

In the rapidly declining market of 2018, five homes in Ocean Park along the same street built by the same developer were for sale between $1.5M - $1.8M. Lance's listings was the first and only sale to complete at $1.75M.

CASE STUDY - INVESTMENT RESULTS

In November 2014 Lance helped a client secure a pre-sale purchase with a 5% deposit of $16,750. The purchase price was $335,000. Four years later after completion the unit was sold for $630,000. That is an annual return of 141% for 3 years on the initial investment.
